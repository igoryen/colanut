About Us

At Nationwide Home Closing Services, our mission is to give you peace of mind during the important - and often stressful - process of closing the purchase, sale or refinancing of your home. With the support of our service and technical teams, and experienced real estate lawyers, we aim to provide a worry-free closing experience, every step of the way.
Nationwide Home Closing Services is an affiliate of The Nationwide Group (TNG), a pioneer in designing and developing outsourced financial services software, exclusively focused on creating comprehensive and customizable solutions to the real estate and mortgage industries. TNG helps financial institutions and the broader lending community manage and mitigate their exposure to inherent business and financial risks.

The Nationwide Group’s affiliated companies also include:

Nationwide Appraisal Services

Nationwide Appraisal Services was established in 1996, and provides lenders with innovative valuation solutions supported by risk analysis, data analytics and product knowledge. Through a successful global expansion, today, NAS does business in more countries than any other AMC in the world, operating in three languages in Canada, the US, Mexico, 15 Caribbean countries, Central America and South America.

Nationwide Recovery Services

Nationwide Recovery Services brings Oversight, Insight and Intelligence to the process of recovering debts, giving financial institutions new capabilities to manage all of the professionals required to turn defaulted collateral into revenue generating assets.

InHouse, Inc.

Founded in 2002, InHouse, Inc. is a financial services technology company offering InHouse Solutions, a full Appraisal Management Company ("AMC"), and InHouse Connexions, an appraisal management software ("AMS") to manage the full administration of the appraisal process, while supporting consistent quality control of policies and procedures.

Our history

The Nationwide Group

1996

NAS

As the founder of TM Appraisers since 1989, Tom McCormick realized that there was a need for lenders to have a central system to manage their mortgage valuations processes. In 1996, he created Nationwide Appraisal Services (NAS), providing fast, reliable and cost-effective valuation services to lenders, using one centralized hub of tools and resources.

2011

Caribbean

Global expansion started in the Caribbean and continues to pioneer its customizable and integrated valuation solutions that simplify the appraisal management process for lenders, mortgage professionals and appraisers.

2012

NRS

Collaborated with MCAP to build Nationwide Recovery Services (NRS). This platform provides financial institutions the oversight, insight and intelligence to recover their debts and turn their defaulted collateral into revenue generating assets. In 2016, The Nationwide Group took full ownership of the NRS platform.

2014

InHouseUSA

Expansion in Latin America with an office establishment in Mexico, and penetration of the US market with the acquisition of InHouseUSA. InHouseUSA supports top and mid-tier lenders with the options to manage their valuation processes ‘in-house’ or outsource the process management with our best-in-class products and services.

2016

HCS

Launch of Nationwide Home Closing Services (HCS). This innovative on-line platform aims to provide a worry-free closing experience, every step of the way, giving home owners peace of mind during the process of closing a purchase, sale or refinancing.
